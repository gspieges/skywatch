How to use SkyWatch
1. Find a flight
Look at the map and click any aircraft. Its callsign appears in the popup (e.g. UAL123).
2. Enter the callsign
Type it into the box at the top and click SET ATC.
3. Listen to ATC
The ATC tab shows the correct frequency for that flight's current phase. Tap the green button to open the live audio stream.
4. Auto-switching
As the flight changes phase (Climb → Cruise → Descent etc.) the recommended frequency updates automatically.
